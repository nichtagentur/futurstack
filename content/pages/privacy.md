## Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 2026**

FuturStack ("we", "us") operates the website futurstack.vercel.app. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data.

### Data Controller

FuturStack Editorial Team
Contact: privacy@futurstack.com

### What Data We Collect

**Automatically collected:**
- Basic server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps)
- These are processed by our hosting provider Vercel and are not stored by us beyond standard server operations

**Cookies:**
- We use a single functional cookie (`cookie-consent`) to remember your cookie preference
- No tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics cookies are used without your explicit consent

### Legal Basis for Processing (DSGVO Art. 6)

- **Legitimate interest** (Art. 6(1)(f)): Server logs for security and site operation
- **Consent** (Art. 6(1)(a)): Any optional cookies or analytics

### Third-Party Services

- **Vercel** (hosting): Processes requests to serve this website. [Vercel Privacy Policy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy)
- **Affiliate partners**: When you click affiliate links, the respective partner may set cookies. Each partner has their own privacy policy.

### Your Rights (DSGVO Art. 15-22)

You have the right to:
- **Access** your personal data
- **Rectification** of inaccurate data
- **Erasure** of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restriction** of processing
- **Data portability**
- **Object** to processing
- **Lodge a complaint** with a supervisory authority

### Affiliate Links

This website contains affiliate links. When you click these links and make a purchase, we may receive a commission. Affiliate partners may use cookies to track referrals. All affiliate links are clearly marked.

### Data Retention

Server logs are retained for a maximum of 30 days. No other personal data is stored.

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

### Contact
