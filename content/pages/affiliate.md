## Affiliate Disclosure

**FuturStack participates in affiliate programs.** This means we may earn a commission when you click on certain links and make a purchase or sign up for a service. This comes at no additional cost to you.

### How It Works

1. We review and recommend AI and SaaS tools based on thorough analysis
2. Some of our links are affiliate links, which are always clearly marked
3. If you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we receive a small commission
4. The commission helps us keep this site running and producing quality content

### Our Editorial Policy

- **Affiliate relationships do not influence our ratings or recommendations.** We recommend tools we believe provide genuine value.
- All affiliate links are clearly identified with "(affiliate link)" labels
- We use `rel="nofollow sponsored"` on all affiliate links as required by search engine guidelines
- Negative reviews and honest assessments are published regardless of affiliate status
- Tools without affiliate programs are reviewed and recommended when they deserve it

### Affiliate Partners

We participate in affiliate programs from various AI and SaaS companies. Our current partners include (but are not limited to):

- Design and creative tools

- Business automation services
