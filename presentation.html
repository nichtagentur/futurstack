The prompt is sent to Claude Sonnet via the Anthropic API. Claude generates a full article with H2/H3 structure, pros/cons, FAQ section, and naturally-placed affiliate shortcodes.

AI-Generated

Automated

The AI Prompt Strategy

The quality of the output depends entirely on the quality of the prompt. Here's what goes in:

What the prompt contains:

  • ✓ Real tool data -- features, pricing tiers, pros, cons (from tools.yaml, not hallucinated)
  • ✓ Target keyword -- e.g., "Jasper AI review 2026"
  • ✓ Article structure -- exact H2/H3 section order
  • ✓ Brand voice -- expert but accessible, "we tested" language
  • ✓ E-E-A-T signals -- first-person experience, specific details
  • ✓ Shortcode placement -- where to put affiliate cards (2-3 per article, natural spots)
  • ✓ Honesty rules -- must mention real limitations

What the output looks like:

## What is Jasper AI?

Jasper AI is a content creation platform built for marketing teams who need to produce high-quality copy at scale. We've been testing Jasper for the past several months, and...

SEO + Legal Compliance

Two things that make or break an affiliate site: search rankings and legal safety.

SEO (getting found on Google)

  • ✓ Schema.org JSON-LD on every page -- Reviews get star ratings in Google results
  • ✓ E-E-A-T signals -- Experience, Expertise, Authority, Trust (Google's quality criteria)
  • ✓ Buyer-intent keywords -- "best X tool 2026", "X vs Y", "X review"
  • ✓ Clean URL structure -- /reviews/jasper-ai/, /comparisons/jasper-vs-copyai/
  • ✓ Auto-generated sitemap.xml + RSS feed
  • ✓ Open Graph + Twitter Cards for social sharing

Legal (staying safe)

  • ✓ FTC disclosure on every article with affiliate links: "This article contains affiliate links..."
  • ✓ rel="nofollow sponsored" on all affiliate links (Google requirement)
  • ✓ DSGVO privacy policy with data processing details
  • ✓ Cookie consent banner -- no tracking until explicit consent
  • ✓ Impressum -- German legal requirement (§ 5 TMG)
  • ✓ AI disclosure in footer: "Content is AI-assisted and expert-reviewed"
